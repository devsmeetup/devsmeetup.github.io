2024-04 Improve Team Performance with Trunk Based Development

24 Apr 19:45

The fourth DevsMeetup Freiburg since the revival. We will meet at 7:45pm. From 8pm Maksym will talk about trunk based development. Free drinks will be provided.

**Description of the talk:**
In today's fast-paced software development world, the quest for efficiency, speed, and quality is never-ending.

The talk is a deep dive into the transformative power of trunk based development practices for teams striving for excellence. We will explore the fundamentals of trunk based development, illustrating how this approach minimizes integration issues, facilitates continuous integration and delivery, and enhances collaboration within teams.
Attendees will gain insights into practical strategies for implementing trunk based development, overcoming common challenges, and measuring its impact on team performance.

Whether you're new to trunk based development or looking to refine your team's practices, this talk will equip you with the knowledge and tools to leverage trunk based development as a catalyst for team performance and project success.

Join us to discover how you can foster a more dynamic, collaborative, and efficient development environment in your organization.
